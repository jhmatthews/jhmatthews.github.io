ejecta_movie.mp4

Animation of the density, pressure, pseudo emissivity, tracer and Lorentz factor from the simulation of a relativistic blob from a stellar mass black hole. The blob decelerates, disrupts due to instabilities and forms a strong reverse shock. It also inflates a low density cavity, overpressured with respect to the ambient medium.
